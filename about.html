The Happy

The Happy Pour-Over Café offers only specialty coffee.

Speciality coffee refers to the whole process from farmer to cup using single origin coffee. And it refers to the way the coffee is roasted and how it is extracted.

They are grown in special and ideal climates and are distinctive because of their full cup taste and little to no defects. The unique flavours and tastes are a result of the special characteristics and composition of the soils in which they are produced. Speciality coffee is quality driven — not like all the other coffees out there. You can pretty much guess at this point that your normal run of the mill coffee in your local grocer would not be considered speciality coffee.

The most obvious difference between speciality coffee shops and other chain store coffee shops are the coffee brewing methods.
